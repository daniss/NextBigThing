## **WHO IS LUCIS? (Key Intelligence)**[^1][^2][^3][^4]

**Lucis** is a French preventive health startup that just raised \$8.5M seed funding led by General Catalyst with participation from Y Combinator, Kima Ventures, and Motier Ventures.

**What They Do**:

- **Comprehensive blood testing**: 2x per year through 400+ partner labs in France, UK, Ireland, Portugal
- **180+ biomarkers tracked**: Cardiovascular, metabolic, hormonal, inflammatory, renal, liver, micronutrient markers
- **AI + medical review**: 5 full-time doctors + AI engine flags risks, trends, anomalies
- **Personalized action plans**: Recommendations in 5 pillars (nutrition, supplements, exercise, sleep, mental health)
- **Dashboard monitoring**: Users track biomarkers over time; wearable integration "coming soon"

**Business Model**: €490/year (~\$535 USD) for 2 annual check-ups

**Traction** (as of December 2025):[^1][^2]

- 3,000 customers
- 500,000+ clinical tests conducted
- 400+ partner laboratories across Europe
- 30,000-person community (WhatsApp/Discord)

**Positioning**: "Europe's Function Health" — positioned as the gap between annual physicals and expensive longevity clinics (\$2K–\$20K/year)

**Founders**: Maxime Berthelot (CEO), Baptiste Debever, Max Guerois

## **WHAT LUCIS DOES WELL vs. GAPS**

### **Lucis's Strengths** ✅

- Clinical rigor (multidisciplinary medical team)
- Comprehensive biomarker panel (180 markers vs. typical 10–20)
- European focus + regulatory compliance (GDPR, HDS-certified)
- Affordable (\$490/year vs. \$2K–\$20K for longevity clinics)
- Strong community (30,000 engaged members)
- Well-funded (\$8.5M) and backed by major VCs


### **Lucis's Gaps** ❌ (Your Opportunities)

- **No wearable integration yet** ("coming soon" but not live) — Users want to see blood biomarkers + sleep + HRV + activity
- **No medical document import** — Users can't digitize old lab reports; history is limited to 6–12 months
- **No genetic data** — Doesn't integrate 23andMe, Ancestry; missing genomic context
- **Limited personalization depth** — Action plans are 5 pillars; no integration with user genetics or lifestyle protocols
- **Biannual testing only** — No continuous/real-time insights; limited trend analysis
- **Centralized cloud** — Not privacy-first; users don't "own" their data

**Verdict**: ⚠️ **Viable if passionate about niche; but slower growth path**

**Verdict**: ✅ **STRONGLY RECOMMENDED**
